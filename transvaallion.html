Transvaal Lion

Transvaal Lions, also known as Kalahari lions, are found in the southern parts of Africa near Swaziland's Hlane Royal national park and South Africa;s Kruger National Park. Majority of the males have a well-developed, black mane. The length of males ranges between 8.5ft and 10.5ft while femaes attain a length between 7.7 feet and 9.0 feet. Male transvaal lions have a weight of about 150-250kg. Female transvaal lions have a weight of 110-182 kg.
