Southwest African Lion

The Southwest African lion, also known as the Katanga lion, is located in southestern Africa in the countries of Angola, Zaire, western Zambia, Zimabwe, Namibia, and nothern Botswana. This subspecies are one of the largest among the other subspecies of lions. Males Katangas can attain lengths between 8.2ft and 10.2ft while females have a length between 7.5ft and 8.7ft. Males weigh around 140-242 kg while females weigh about 105-170 kg. The manes are a lighter color compared to the other subspecies.
